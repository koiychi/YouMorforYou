About this tutorial
Java is a popular programming language widely used in a lot of applications — from mobile apps to websites. Java's powerful capabilty is almost everywhere! Thus, learning it is very crucial as it may land you on your very dream of becoming a successful programmer. We believe that experience is the best teacher. With this tutorial course, we will teach you the basics of Java in an easy and enjoyable way. Just enjoy learning the course, try your capabilities with our Code Diving activities with "Code Dive" — integrated with JDoodle IDE for your experience, and assess your learnings with our quizes at the end of this course.

Are you ready to bring out the best in you? Prepare to be amazed!
